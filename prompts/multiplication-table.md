---
title: Multiplication Table
difficulty: Easy
---

Write a function that prints a formatted multiplication table up to N×N.

For `N = 4`, the output should look something like:

```
 1  2  3  4
 2  4  6  8
 3  6  9 12
 4  8 12 16
```

- Numbers should be right-aligned so columns line up cleanly regardless of N.

**Part 2** — Accept a second argument to print only a specific row or column.
